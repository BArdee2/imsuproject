About Us

At Serenity Counseling, we provide a safe, non-judgmental space for you to explore life's challenges. Our licensed professionals use evidence-based therapies to help you cope with stress, anxiety, trauma, and more.

We believe in the power of therapy to transform lives. Our approach is client-centered, focusing on your unique needs and goals to help you achieve lasting positive change.

With over 15 years of experience, our team is dedicated to providing the highest quality care in a warm and welcoming environment.

Our Services

Individual Therapy

Personalized one-on-one counseling to help manage stress, depression, and life transitions. We work with you to develop coping strategies and build resilience.

Couples Counseling

Supportive sessions to rebuild trust, improve communication, and strengthen relationships. We help couples navigate challenges and deepen their connection.

Teen Support

Helping adolescents navigate peer pressure, identity, school stress, and emotional health. We provide a safe space for teens to express themselves and develop coping skills.
